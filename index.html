SOIL SYSTEM

Know Your Soil

Understanding your soil type is the foundation of successful farming. Each soil has unique properties that support different crops.

🏜️

Sandy Soil

Low Water Retention Warms Quickly pH: 5.5–7.0

Light and well-draining. Good for root vegetables and drought-tolerant crops.

Best Crops:
GroundnutWatermelonCarrotRadishMillet
Tips:
  • Add organic compost to improve fertility
  • Frequent but light irrigation needed
  • Apply mulch to retain moisture
🧱

Clay Soil

High Water Retention Slow to Warm pH: 6.0–7.5

Heavy and nutrient-rich but can become waterlogged. Ideal for paddy.

Best Crops:
RiceWheatCottonSugarcaneCabbage
Tips:
  • Add sand/grit to improve drainage
  • Avoid working when wet
  • Deep ploughing helps aeration
🌱

Loamy Soil

Balanced Drainage Ideal for Farming pH: 6.0–7.0

Best all-purpose soil. Rich in nutrients with good texture for most crops.

Best Crops:
WheatMaizeVegetablesFruitsPulses
Tips:
  • Maintain with regular compost
  • Ideal for crop rotation
  • Minimal soil amendments needed
⚫

Black Soil

High Shrink-Swell Mineral Rich pH: 7.5–8.5

Also called regur soil. Excellent for cotton due to high moisture retention.

Best Crops:
CottonSoybeanSunflowerSorghumLentils
Tips:
  • Needs drainage channels
  • Cracks in summer – good for aeration
  • Low nitrogen – add urea

Soil Testing Guide

When to Test?

  • Before each crop season
  • After heavy rainfall or flood
  • Every 2-3 years minimum

What to Check?

  • NPK (Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium)
  • pH level and Organic Carbon
  • Secondary nutrients: Ca, Mg, S

How to Test?

  • Visit nearest Soil Health Centre
  • Use Soil Health Card (Gov. India)
  • Private lab testing available

WATER MANAGEMENT

Water Scarcity Solutions

💧

Drip Irrigation

Delivers water directly to roots. Saves 30-50% water compared to flood irrigation.

85% Efficient
  • Ideal for vegetables, fruits, cotton
  • Reduces weed growth
  • Govt. subsidy available up to 90%
🌧️

Rainwater Harvesting

Collect and store monsoon rainwater for dry season use.

70% Effective
  • Farm ponds – low cost solution
  • Check dams for groundwater recharge
  • Reduces dependency on borewells
🚿

Sprinkler System

Mimics natural rainfall. Ideal for uneven terrain and dense crops.

75% Efficient
  • Good for wheat, groundnut, vegetables
  • Can also apply fertilizers via water
  • Reduces soil erosion
🌱

Mulching

Cover soil with organic material to retain moisture and reduce evaporation.

60% Water Saved
  • Use straw, dried leaves, plastic sheets
  • Reduces watering frequency
  • Controls weed growth too
